# Logistics
*Who is responsible for this?* \\
This coffee is co-hosted by Hannah, Saul, Claire, and Rhea.

*Can I bring my pet?* \\
Only if it stays outside (on porch, front yard, no fence). \\
If you have allergies, be aware that there are two cats in the house, but they will be kept in a separate room. 

*Can I bring my kids?* \\
Yes!

*Can I bring my friends?* \\
Yes!

*What else can I bring?* \\
Nothing! We want this to be easy. 

*Will there be food?* \\
We'll have coffee and tea and light snacks. Feel free to bring your own.

*What is the seating like?*\\
Tables, chairs, couches, floor. There is also a porch.

*What will we do?* \\
Cozy up by the electric "fireplace," get work done, or you can bring crafts or games.

*Are there bathrooms? Wifi? Outlets?* \\
Yes! 

*What about covid protections?* \\
You are welcome to mask! We will have an air filter running inside.
